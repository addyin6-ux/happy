# Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.
- **Data collected**: phone, PAN, Aadhaar, voter ID, bank account, credit score.
- **Purpose**: KYC, credit assessment, loan matching, compliance.
- **Sharing**: shared with selected NBFCs/banks only with explicit consent.
- **Storage**: encrypted at rest; access limited to authorized personnel.
- **Rights**: access, correction, deletion requests supported.
- **Contact**: privacy@yourdomain.example
